** Discord crypto and stock tracker (in developpement)** 

This bot has been developped to track stocks and crypto assets on discord.

It has been initially developped on replit.com and uses its db.

It uses : 
  - Alphavantage API to get prices for crypto and stocks (https://www.alphavantage.co/, API key is free)
  - Replit DB to stock Watchlists

!help :
 • !yo : says Yo
 • !booba : an inspirational Booba quote
 • !price : retrieves stock price and growth
 • !cryptoprice : retrieves crypto price and growth
 • !stocks : displays your stock watchlist
 • !stocks add : adds a stock to your watchlist
 • !stocks delete : removes a stock from your watchlist
 • !stocks reset : resets your stock watchlist
 • !cryptolist : displays your cryptowatchlist
 • !cryptolist reset : resets your cryptowatchlist
 • !cryptolist add : adds a crypto to tour watchlist
 • !cryptolist delete : removes a crypto from your watchlist
